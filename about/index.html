About

This website is a project of Doug Belshaw. After being concerned about the impact we've been having on the Earth for a while, it was reading Jem Bendell's paper Deep Adaptation: A Map for Navigating Climate Tragedy that prompted me to start trying to think more deeply about the end of our species.

I tend to make sense of the world through writing and documenting, so I thought I'd set up this simple site as a way to grasp the impact of impending human exinction on my own life. It may also be useful as a place to point others who may find this kind of resource useful or provocative.

You can get in touch at: contact@extinction.fyi
